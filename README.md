### 3.1 Your Statement of Intent

I will be creating a reliable and accurate website about the Japanese anime Attack on Titan. It will be easily accessible, as it is designed for every fan of the series. It will feature a short synopsis of the series, as well as a more comprehensive timeline of the chronological events in the story. The website will also feature extensive character profiles of key characters, as well as a small forum where fans can interact and share their opinions. Subsidiary pages of other characters, items, and places will also be included.

The website Fandom continually spreads content that is inaccurate and unreliable, since it is a public website where anyone can add anything. This website, however, is accessible to the public but a certain process needs to be implemented before someone can become an editor. This ensures that all information is reliable and accurate.

This website will ensure that information about the series will encourage people to collaborate on one specific project tailored to their interests. It will also be a convenient database for fans of the series that simply want more information on a specific topic. This website will also provide users a way to anonymously interact with each other while discussing the Attack on Titan series.

This website is designed specifically for fans of Attack on Titan, most specifically people 15 to 30 years old. This website would foster a sense of community and geek culture among fans, weebs and anyone that is interested in Attack on Titan. However, many of the articles contain spoilers for the anime or manga, so in order to have the best experience, people should have watched or read Attack on Titan at least once.

This website is designed for fans of Attack on Titan as a reference point for people who either want to get back into the series or just to research major plot points. This website will be successful when it presents the content clearly, with a simple interface and colours.

### 3.3 Evaluation Against Your Intent (2–3 paragraphs)

> Take each aim in your Statement of Intent and evaluate **how well the
> delivered site meets it**. Where did you meet your intent? Where did you fall
> short, and why? Support every judgement with evidence from your site.

I stated in my Statement of Intent, that I would create a website where every fan would be able to access accurate and reliable information about Attack on Titan while keeping it accessible by using readable fonts and contrasting colours to make it accessible to the general public. This website fulfilled this by not only having a library that acts as a database for information, but with the entire website having a consistent colour scheme that doesn't overwhelm readers. This could be enhanced by adding more colour while still retaining contrast, as the design currently looks plain and not eye-catching.

I also stated that I will implement a small forum, and editor permissions in my statement of intent. Sadly, I failed to implement these features in my website. I would not only use these features that I intended, but upgrade them by making it so only certain accounts can edit, and having a feature that bans users that post harmful content on the platform. This will also be complemented by a reply feature, and users will also gain the ability to freely censor parts of their messages due to spoilers.

### 3.4 Overall Effectiveness (1–2 paragraphs)

> Step back from the detail. Overall, **how effective** is the website at
> achieving its purpose for its target audience? Weigh what works against what
> falls short, and state what you would improve to better meet your intent.

This website achieves the core purpose stated in my Statement of Intent, and it fits the target audience of my website. It fulfills the core purpose of presenting information about Attack on Titan in a legible way that fits the target audience (teenagers/young adults) by creating articles, with a working search bar for more specific searches. However, the layout is rather lackluster, with the navbar only being in black and white.
I would improve my website in general by adding more colour to the layout and adding a comment feature so users can interact with each other.
